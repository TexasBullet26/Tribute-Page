Remembering a Life

Life of Chester Bennington:
“Your voice was joy and pain, anger and forgiveness, love and heartache all wrapped up into one. I suppose that’s what we all are. You helped me understand that.”
- Chester Bennington
(after Chris Cornell's death)
  • On October 24, 2000, Chester and Linkin Park released their debut album, Hybrid Theory
  • Musical influences include: Depeche Mode, The Cure, The Misfits, Fugazi, Minor Threat, The Smiths, Skinny Puppy, Nitzer Ebb, Ministry, x.
  • Also performed as vocalist for Stone Temple Pilots from 2013 to 2015
  • Has six children, Jamie (21), Isaiah (20), Draven (15), Tyler (11), twins Lilly and Lila (6)
  • On July 20, 2017 Bennington commited suicide by hanging at his home in Palos Verdes Estates, California.
